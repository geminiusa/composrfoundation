Welcome to Composr Foundation.

Thank you for visiting the Composr Foundation Github Repository. The new home for Composr Themes, Graphics and Add ons. .

Theme Foundations

We think that variety is natural desire and want to provide choices when it comes to Web Design and the Composr Portal. We will be working with a variety of Frameworks to bring exciting themes for your ocPortal and Composr Portals. You will find that we try to stay up to date and know what works for your website. We will be using Bootstrap and Foundation Frameworks as a base for our themes. As well as html5 and CSS3 layouts as well..

Joining our Project

If you like what we are doing and would like to join the team please feel free to contact us at your earliest convenience as we are always looking for team mates with eagerness and a thriving imagination that wants to help provide themes for this great community. Visit us at Composr Designs.
